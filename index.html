About PCGsoft

PCGsoft Ltd is a UK software company building practical, focused tools for real operational problems. The current portfolio centres on transport compliance, fleet operations, AI-assisted workflows and community-focused mobile applications.

The aim is simple: create software that is useful, understandable and reliable enough for people who need to get work done, not just software that looks good in a demo.
